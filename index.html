Game Development

Epistasis

Epistasis is a horror game where you explore an abandoned space station, trying to find out what happened there.

It was developed by a group of students from Business College Helsinki in the span of ~4 months, from January 2023 to May 2023.

My contribution to the project consisted of Level design, 3D modeling, animating and general programming.
